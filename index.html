HEY THERE, I'M KATIE.

I'm a senior at the University of Pennsylvania's School of Engineering and Applied Science, currently pursuing a BSE degree in the Digital Media Design program, and an MSE in Computer Graphics & Game Technology. I'm interested in computer graphics, computer animation, computer vision, and not to brag, but I'm an expert parallel parker.

CODE

I do iOS, front-end, and distributed systems in Obj-C/Swift, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Java/C++. I specialize in computer graphics so 3D programming is kind of my jam.

DESIGN

I have experience in UI/UX, IxD, and prototyping, using tools like Sketch, Adobe Ps/Ai, and Framer.js to create a pixel perfect experience.

FUN
