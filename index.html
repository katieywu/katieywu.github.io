HEY THERE, I'M KATIE.

I'm a senior at the University of Pennsylvania's School of Engineering and Applied Science, currently pursuing a BSE degree in the Digital Media Design program, and an MSE in Computer Graphics & Game Technology. I'm interested in tech (obviously), VR/AR, design, baking the perfect lemon cake and aspiring to brew the perfect cup of Earl Grey tea. Oh and I'm an excellent parallel parker.

CODE

I have experience and interest in iOS, front-end, and distributed systems using Obj-C/Swift, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Java/C++. I also do computer graphics.

DESIGN

I do UI/UX, IxD, and prototyping, using tools like Sketch, Adobe Ps/Ai, and Framer.js to create pixel perfect experiences.

FUN

I'm passionate about 3D modeling, virtual and augmented reality, painting, and getting my hands dirty (literally) with pottery and painting.
