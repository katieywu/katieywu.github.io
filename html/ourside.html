Ourside

The Gist: Ourside is a Microsoft Hololens app integrated with a Leap Motion controller that allows you to draw in augmented reality using just your fingertips. The name Ourside came from an idea we had about creating a new persistent world on top of our existing one, where each physical space becomes a new medium for shared art and creation.

Developed with Nick Newberg, and Alex Daley-Montgomery in Unity3D Engine
